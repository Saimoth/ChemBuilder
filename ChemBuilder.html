ChemBuilder

Add: drag C, H or O from the tray.

Bond: move two atoms close together. Move them closer again to increase a single bond to double, then triple.

Strengthen a bond: double-tap an atom. Its eligible bonded neighbours are selected in turn, upgrading single → double → triple.

Skeletal view: the panel in the top-right hides carbon labels and carbon-bound hydrogens, so you can learn the line-angle formula while building.

Challenge mode: build the requested compound. The game checks automatically after each change and advances after a correct answer.

Delete: drag an atom back into the tray.

The name is determined from the complete connection pattern. C₂H₆O can therefore be recognised as ethanol or dimethyl ether.
